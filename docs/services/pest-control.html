What We Do
Our Pest Control Services Services
01
Integrated Pest Management (IPM) Solutions

Science-based IPM methodology that combines preventive, monitoring, and corrective interventions — minimising chemical use while maximising effectiveness.

02
Customised Treatment Plans

No two facilities are alike. We design site-specific treatment plans based on pest risk profile, facility type, and occupancy patterns.

03
Preventive & Corrective Pest Control

Scheduled preventive treatments with AMC contracts, plus rapid-response corrective interventions when infestations are detected.

04
Compliance, Documentation & Reporting

WHO and CIB-approved products. Treatment certificates issued after every visit — audit-ready for FSSAI, NABH, or any regulatory inspection.
