Who We Are

Built to fix the fragmentation problem

BelaBloom Projects & Facility Management Pvt. Ltd. was incorporated with the Ministry of Commerce Affairs on 27th April 2021, registered at the Registrar of Companies (ROC), Maharashtra.

We exist because most businesses waste enormous time and money coordinating with multiple vendors for things that should come from one place — a real estate broker, an interior contractor, a housekeeping agency, a pest control company, a fire safety vendor, and an insurance advisor, all separately, all with misaligned incentives.

Belabloom aggregates the entire infrastructure lifecycle under one roof. We are operationally accountable — we hold vendor contracts, supervise execution, and sign off on quality. We have obtained all required compliance certificates to perform our operations across India.

We have successfully partnered with leading organisations across Financial Services, General Insurance, IT & ITES, Mining, and Cash Management sectors.

Meet the Team
Mr. Ashok Singh
Founder & Managing Director

Ashok Singh founded BelaBloom on 21st April 2021, bringing over 30 years of experience in Projects Management, Estate Management, Procurement, Facilities Management and Operations.

He began his career at IIT Delhi in 1990, progressing through Dalmia Industries, Pertech Computers, NIIT, NIS Sparta, and Reliance. As Group Manager at Bharti Airtel (2006–2008), and then Deputy Vice President at Edelweiss Securities Limited from 2008, he led the establishment of 400+ branch offices across India and Singapore, implementing ISO-9001:2000, ISO27001 (ISMS), and Oracle Property Management.

He holds an MBA in Project Management earned while working at Edelweiss.

Mr. Anil Shukla
Project Consultant

Anil Shukla is a committed professional with over 25 years of varied experience in Project Management, Facility Management, Administration, and Civil Construction with a specialisation in MEP functions.

He previously held senior roles at Edelweiss and the Sahara Group, gaining extensive exposure in P&L Management, Leadership, and Strategy.

He has demonstrated capabilities in achieving top-line growth and bottom-line improvements, with strong analytical skills and key decision-making abilities to secure profitable businesses.
