Our Services

At Belabloom Projects & Facility Management, we provide end-to-end business solutions designed to streamline operations, enhance workplace efficiency, and create safer, more productive environments. Explore our comprehensive range of services below.

Sourcing of Business Spaces

  • Finding the right commercial space can define the future of your business.
    We assist you in identifying, evaluating, and securing business spaces that fit your operational, financial, and strategic needs.
    Whether it’s an office, retail outlet, warehouse, or facility, we ensure every location aligns with your vision.

    Our services include:

    • Space identification & shortlisting

    • Site visits & evaluations

    • Market analysis & pricing insights

    • Negotiation support

    • Documentation guidance

Pest Control Services

Ensure a clean, hygienic, and pest-free environment for your workplace.
Our professional pest control services are safe, effective, and tailored to your business requirements.

We handle:

  • Cockroach & ant control

  • Rodent control

  • Termite treatment (pre & post construction)

  • Mosquito & fly control

  • Commercial pest management plans (AMC)

Interior Setup & Project Management

Transform your space into a functional, aesthetic, and brand-aligned environment.
Our interior setup and project management solutions take care of the entire lifecycle—from design planning to final execution.

We offer:

  • Concept design & layout planning

  • Modular furniture & workstation setup

  • Civil, electrical, and plumbing work

  • IT infrastructure setup

  • On-site project supervision

  • Quality checks & timely delivery

Facility Management

Create a workspace that operates smoothly every day.
Our integrated facility management services ensure operational efficiency, reduced downtime, and a healthy work environment.

Core services include:

  • Housekeeping & cleaning management

  • Technical maintenance (HVAC, electrical, plumbing, DG sets)

  • Vendor & asset management

  • Security & support staff

  • Soft and hard services management

Audit & Processes

Improve efficiency with well-defined systems and transparent processes.
We help organisations streamline workflows through structured audits and actionable process recommendations.

Areas we cover:

  • Operational audits

  • Facility audits

  • Compliance checks

  • Process GAP analysis

  • SOP creation & implementation

Fire Safety Equipment

Safety is non-negotiable.
We supply and install certified fire safety equipment that protects your business, employees, and assets from potential hazards.

Our fire safety solutions include:

  • Fire extinguishers (supply & refilling)

  • Fire alarm systems

  • Hydrant & sprinkler systems

  • Emergency exit signage

  • Annual maintenance contracts (AMC)
